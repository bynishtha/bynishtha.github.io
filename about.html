Meet the Artist

Nishtha, lovingly known as Bubu, turns yarn into magic with each crochet creation. Every item is handmade with love, warmth, and creativity.

Nishtha's passion for crocheting began with a simple hook and a heart full of dreams. Inspired by cozy aesthetics, anime, and all things cute, she brings her imagination to life through handcrafted pieces.

Whether it’s a tiny flower, a cuddly Totoro, or a custom plushie, each item carries her love and care—ready to brighten someone's day.
